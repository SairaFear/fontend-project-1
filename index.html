Ray-Ban sunglasses have long been a cultural and fashion staple, known for their iconic design and unparalleled quality.

Established in 1937 by Bausch & Lomb, Ray-Ban originally produced sunglasses for U.S. Army Air Corps pilots.

The company’s goal was to create eyewear that would reduce the glare from the sun’s intense rays while maintaining a clear, unobstructed field of vision.
